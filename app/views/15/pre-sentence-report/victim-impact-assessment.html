CPS pack

Help with victim impact assessment

Summarise the information here so that it can be used to inform sentencing options later in the report. This may include things like exclusion zones and can feed into the risk of serious harm.

Do not simply restate the facts from the CPS report, or rely solely on the account provided in the pre-sentence report interview.

You may want to consider the defendant's:

  • attitudes
  • responsibility
  • circumstances
  • motivations
