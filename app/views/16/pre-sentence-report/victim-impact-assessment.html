Victim impact assessment

The victim impact assessment is used to inform sentencing options such as exclusion zones. You will be able to review this later as you are writing your sentencing proposal.

View CPS pack

Help with victim impact assessment

The CPS pack usually includes a statement from the victim. Use this alongside the account provided in the pre-sentence interview to write the victim impact assessment. This should consider the defendant's:

  • attitudes
  • responsibility
  • circumstances
  • motivations
