Four step process of risk assessment

The structured framework is a step-by-step process to help you make a decision about whether someone poses Low, Medium, High or Very High risk of serious harm. It uses actuarial risk predictor scores and professional assessment.

Four step process:

  • Predictors
  • Risk factors and protective factors
  • Immediacy (and likelihood?)
  • Assign the risk level
