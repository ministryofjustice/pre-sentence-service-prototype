A verification report is a new kind of pre-sentence advice. This information will help you complete this research task.

What is a verification report?

A verification report is a way to decide whether someone is suitable for either unpaid work or an electronic monitoring curfew.

What are the criteria for a verification report?

Someone can be considered for a verification report if they have entered a guilty anticipated plea, have no risk flags in NDelius and something else.
