Start page

A verification report is a new kind of pre-sentence advice. This information will help you complete this research task.

What is a verification report?

When someone meets the criteria for a verification report, you will:

  • request domestic violence checks from the police
  • request child safeguarding checks from social services
  • interview the defendant before their first hearing

Using the information from the police checks and interview, you can assess someone's suitability for either electronic monitoring curfew, unpaid work or both.

You can deliver this information to the court in a defendant's plea hearing. The court will not need to adjourn for a pre-sentence report to be prepared.

What are the criteria for a verification report?

Someone can be considered for a verification report if:

  • their most serious offence is in scope for a community order but not severe enough for a custodial sentence
  • they have entered a guilty anticipated plea
  • they have no specific risk flags in NDelius
  • there are no case markers in Common Platform
  • they are at liberty on the day of their plea hearing

Defendants from black and ethnic minority backgrounds, female defendants and those aged between 18 to 25 years old are not eligible for verification reports. They must receive a full pre-sentence report.

There are also times when a sentencer may request a verification report either before or after a plea hearing, or after a trial. A probation officer may also assess someone for a pre-sentence report and decide that they are more suitable for a verification report.

This service will identify cases which meet these criteria automatically. You must confirm if it has identified cases correctly, and identified all eligible cases by confirming each defendant's suitability for either a verification report, a pre-sentence report or no advice.

How to use this service

You can complete this form in any order, over a period of time.

You should add information as you get it. We know that some of the checks might not come back quickly, you should save a draft and complete the missing information when you get it.
